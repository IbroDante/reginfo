About

About J.A.M Ltd

JODOR Asset Management (J.A.M) Ltd, is a 21st century forward-looking asset management company founded on a rock-solid foundation to unlock value in Africa's most dynamic market.

The forum will bring together industry leaders, policymakers, investors, and innovators to discuss the future of sustainable energy in Africa. With the continent's energy demands growing rapidly, SEF-2025 aims to foster collaboration and showcase cutting-edge solutions.

We are a widely known brand in the multi-strategy asset management arena and have come a long way from our initial founding to our business restructuring in recent years and beyond.

Our Business Focus Include

  • Commodity Asset Management
  • Technology Asset Management
  • Infrastructure Asset Management
  • Human Capital Asset Management
  • Aqua Marine Asset Management
  • Investment Research and Project Execution
  • Trade Advisory and Consulting

We apply cutting-edge research, machine learning, artificial intelligence, and advanced technology to drive sustainable growth and development on critical projects.

We aim to be Africa's premier asset management firm, driving innovation across key critical sectors, and delivering unparalleled value to our clients, and we are a dedicated team of professionals with vast experience across multiple cities, countries and jurisdictions, and we work head over heels in the prompt delivery and the swift execution of project deliverables across major sectors.

We pride ourselves in time (T) based results, using the PIE methodology.

Forum Highlights

The Sustainable Energy Forum 2025 will feature:

  • Keynote addresses from industry pioneers
  • Panel discussions on renewable energy integration
  • Technology showcase of innovative solutions
  • Networking opportunities with investors and policymakers
  • Workshops on sustainable energy financing

"We're at a critical juncture for Africa's energy future. SEF-2025 will provide the platform needed to accelerate our transition to sustainable energy solutions,"

Dr. (Engr.) Nduka Ofulue,
MD/CEO J.A.M Ltd.
